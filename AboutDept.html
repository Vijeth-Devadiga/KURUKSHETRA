ABOUT THE DEPARTMENTS

Department Of Commerce

The Department of Commerce, one of the most dynamic and distinguished departments of the college, takes immense pride in co-organizing the intercollegiate PU-level competition “Kurukshetra - Where the Warriors Meet.” Renowned for its dedicated faculty and a vibrant community of aspiring students, the department has consistently been a pillar of excellence and innovation since the college's inception.

With a strong commitment to providing practical exposure, holistic development, and industry-oriented learning, the Commerce Department continues to shape students into confident, skilled, and career-ready professionals who excel in every arena they enter.

Department of Computer Applications

The Department of Computer Applications, a hub of innovation and technology-driven learning, proudly joins hands in organizing the intercollegiate PU-level competition “Kurukshetra - Where the Warriors Meet.” Known for its enthusiastic learners and technically proficient faculty, the department fosters creativity, logical thinking, and a passion for solving real-world problems through technology.

With a vision to nurture future-ready IT professionals, the Computer Application Association focuses on practical exposure, collaborative learning, and skill development, empowering students to become competent, confident, and innovative contributors to the digital world.
